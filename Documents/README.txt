----Procedurally Generated 3D Platformer Level----

Created using Godot and C#.


Instructions:

1) Open controls.png to read the game's controls.

2) Open the application "PCG 3D Platformer Level" to play the game.

3) Select what attributes you desire for the generated level.

4) Select "Start" and play and complete the level.



Missing:

* Enemy AI. This fortunately does not affect the layout of the level.
* Ranking system due to time constraints. This hopefully will be added in a build during the middle of May, please let me know if you are interested in playing that build.
* Time bonus due to time constraints.
